The main project

Curriculum Planner

A desktop application that helps you turn the Australian Curriculum into something you can teach from: clear learning statements, assessment items, and knowledge tracking, built up step by step.

  • From curriculum to classroom. Helps you translate official curriculum documents into a workable program.
  • Assessment built in. Every learning statement is paired with a way to measure it.
  • Grounded in research. Includes background resources on curriculum principles and common planning problems.
Learn more at cp.learningnow.com.au

It helps you answer two questions

  1. What do you want students to know and be able to do?
  2. How will you know they've learned it?
